Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Podcast Impact Studio ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use Podcast Impact Content Studio (the "App"). We've written this in plain English so it's actually readable. If you have questions, email us at info@podcastimpactstudio.com.

What We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your business or organization name
  • Your timezone
  • Your password (encrypted — we never see it in plain text)

Show DNA & Content

When you use the App, we store:

  • Show profiles you create (name, voice, audience details, platforms, boilerplate)
  • Episode transcripts you paste or upload for content generation
  • Generated content packages (show notes, social posts, etc.)

Transcripts are sent to Anthropic's API for processing and are subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy. We do not permanently store transcripts — they are processed in real time and not saved to our database.

Usage Data

We may collect basic usage information such as:

  • Pages and features accessed within the App
  • Browser type and device information
  • Error logs to help us fix bugs

How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the App
  • Generate AI content packages using your show's DNA
  • Send transactional emails (account confirmation, password reset)
  • Process payments and manage your subscription
  • Respond to support requests
We do not sell your data. We do not use your show content or transcripts to train AI models.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

🔐 Supabase

Handles user authentication, account data, and database storage. Your account information and show profiles are stored securely in Supabase. Supabase Privacy Policy →

🤖 Anthropic (Claude AI)

Powers all AI content generation. Episode transcripts you submit are sent to Anthropic's API for processing. Anthropic does not use API inputs to train their models by default. Anthropic Privacy Policy →

📁 Google Drive (Optional)

If you choose to connect your Google account, we request permission to create files in your Google Drive. We only access files created by this App and do not read, modify, or delete any other files in your Drive. Google Privacy Policy →

💳 Stripe (Payments)

Handles subscription billing. We do not store your credit card information — all payment data is managed directly by Stripe. Stripe Privacy Policy →

🚀 Vercel

Hosts the App. Vercel may collect server logs and performance data. Vercel Privacy Policy →

Data Security

We take data security seriously:

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS (encrypted in transit)
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
  • Row-level security ensures each account can only access its own data
  • API keys and service credentials are stored securely as environment variables, never in client-side code

Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal information and show data will be removed from our systems within 30 days.

Transcripts are not stored after processing — they exist only in memory during generation and are discarded immediately after.

Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — Update or correct your personal information
  • Deletion — Request that we delete your account and data
  • Portability — Request your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out — Disconnect Google Drive or other integrations at any time from Settings

To exercise any of these rights, email us at info@podcastimpactstudio.com.

Cookies

The App uses minimal cookies — primarily session tokens to keep you logged in. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels. You can clear cookies at any time through your browser settings, though this will log you out of the App.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for anyone under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we'll notify users by email.
